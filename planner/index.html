STEP 03 Compute Deployment

Choose your analytical approach — review past coverage retrospectively, or plan future deployment prospectively. The optimizer then computes station locations and unit counts for your selected approach.

Analytical Approach

How should we have deployed for past incidents? Useful for after-action review or known fixed events.

How should we plan deployment for expected future demand? Useful for annual planning and procurement decisions.

Method

Plan a robust deployment for the historical demand pattern, with optional growth scaling for expected demand increase.

Validate the deployment with statistical confidence intervals across multiple synthetic future scenarios. Useful for procurement defense.

Direct Fit places stations around the exact incidents loaded in this session. It is useful for forensic review, fixed-event planning, and explaining how a known historical period would have been covered. It is not suitable for procurement planning or annual deployment sizing because it can overfit isolated calls, missing records, and random historical noise. For station investment decisions, use Smoothed Demand + Growth or Monte Carlo.
Advanced
Expected growth in incident volume over the planning period. Factored into unit sizing and recommendations; station placement stays anchored to today's demand pattern. Use +0% for steady state, higher for growing demand.
